About Us

Your trusted guide to retiring in the Land of Smiles

Our Mission

At Retiring in Thailand, our mission is to provide accurate, comprehensive, and up-to-date information to help people make informed decisions about retiring in Thailand. We understand that relocating to a new country is a life-changing decision, and we're here to make that journey smoother.

Our Story

Retiring in Thailand was founded by expats who went through the process themselves. We know firsthand the challenges of navigating visa requirements, finding the right location, understanding healthcare options, and adapting to a new culture.

After successfully making Thailand our home, we decided to share our knowledge and experiences to help others achieve their dream of retiring in this beautiful country. What started as a simple blog has grown into a comprehensive resource trusted by thousands of aspiring and current expats.
