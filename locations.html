Thailand's original beach resort

Tips to help you make the best decision

üß≥ Try Before You Commit

  • Spend 1-3 months in each location
  • Rent short-term accommodations first
  • Experience both high and low seasons
  • Join local expat groups for insights

üè• Healthcare Priorities

  • Check hospital proximity
  • Verify specialist availability
  • Consider ongoing health needs
  • Bangkok is best for serious care

üå°Ô∏è Climate Matters

  • Chiang Mai: Cooler, but smoky season
  • Islands: Great beaches, monsoon rains
  • Bangkok: Hot year-round
  • Visit during worst season to test

üë• Community Connection

  • Find expat groups on Facebook
  • Attend meetups before moving
  • Consider language classes
  • Look for shared hobbies/interests
