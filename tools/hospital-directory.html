ğŸ¥ Health Insurance for Expats

Health insurance is required for the retirement visa and highly recommended for all expats.

Required Coverage

  • Minimum $100,000 USD coverage required for O-A visa
  • Must cover COVID-19 treatment
  • Outpatient coverage of at least $40,000
  • Inpatient coverage of at least $100,000

Popular Providers

  • AXA (Thailand)
  • BUPA (Thailand)
  • Pacific Cross
  • Cigna Global
  • Allianz Worldwide

Cost Estimates

  • Age 50-59: $100-200/month
  • Age 60-69: $180-350/month
  • Age 70+: $300-600/month
  • Pre-existing conditions: higher
